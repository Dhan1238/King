CARBON DIOXIDE
CO2 is non toxic and non combustible. It is a colorless gas with a density about 60% higher than that of dry air. Carbon dioxide consists of a carbon atom covalently double bonded to two oxygen atoms. It occurs naturally in Earth's atmosphere as a trace gas.


Application
Aerated beverages & breweries.
Metal fabrication
Foundries
Fire extinguisher
Water and effluent treatment
